GUIDE Audio Sync Guide
  1. 1
    Choose a source
    Upload a file (mp3/wav), use your mic, or tap VIDEO to use the video's own audio
  2. 2
    Enable sync
    Turn Sync ON, then pick a target: MIX, QTY, VAR, HUE, FLASH, PULSE, or RATE
  3. 3
    Pick a band
    Kick (bass hits), Vocal (mids), Hats (highs), or Full — each responds to different parts of the sound
  4. 4
    Tune reactivity
    Sensitivity = intensity, Threshold = gate quiet parts, Release = decay speed
  5. 5
    Per-effect sync
    Open any active effect → Audio Sync toggle → map a specific parameter to a band
  6. 6
    Check the overview
    The Per-Effect Audio Sync section below shows all active mappings at a glance

PROJECTION GUIDE
Simple Setup (flat surface)
1. Connect projector as second display
2. Click PROJECTION WINDOW (or Shift+P)
3. Drag the window to your projector display
4. Press F in the projection window for fullscreen
5. Control effects from this screen — visuals play on the projector
With MadMapper / Resolume
1. Open the Projection Window
2. In MadMapper: Add Input → Window Capture
3. Select "H.O.D. — Projection" from the window list
4. Map the captured feed onto your surfaces
For lower latency: OBS → capture window → Syphon output → MadMapper receives the feed
Audio-Reactive Projection
1. Go to Audio tab → set source to MIC
2. Enable Audio Sync and choose effects to sync
3. Open the Projection Window
4. Your visuals now react to live sound through the projector
Controls
Shift+P — toggle projection window

Features

Effects — 68 effects across Color, Distortion, Pattern, and Overlay categories. Click to apply, eye to toggle, trash to remove.
Audio Sync — The heart of H.O.D. Connect sound to almost everything. Global sync targets + per-effect parameter mapping. Look for the ♫ badge.
Tracking — Color, Analysis, and AI detection families. 19 modes including AI Mask, Face, Eyes, Lips, and Background Subtraction.
Region FX — Apply GPU effects (blur, thermal, glitch, etc.) directly to tracked blobs.
Overlays — Drag a video or image onto the canvas. 12 blend modes, opacity control, cover/contain/stretch fit.
Layers — Manage active effects with eye toggles, opacity, and blend modes. Scene layer controls video/effects/blobs.
Presets — Curated effect combos. Save your own custom presets to revisit later.
Timeline — Sequence tracking modes, effects, and audio sync over time. Drag to add, edges to trim.
